calc/adele

calc/adele is a calculator for minimalist on finite points. $n \backslash r$ means $r \bmod n$. For example, $2^8 3^3 7 \backslash 7 / 2^{12} 3 \in (\mathbb{Z}_2 \times \mathbb{Z}_3 \times \mathbb{Z}_7) \otimes \mathbb{Q}$ with following accuracies:
  • $\mathbb{Z}_2$ : $8 \log 2$
  • $\mathbb{Z}_3$ : $3 \log 3$
  • $\mathbb{Z}_7$ : $\log 7$

calc/arch

calc/arch is a calculator for minimalist at infinity point. calc/arch recognizes theory of relativity and quantum mechanics via $c = \hbar = G = 1$. It adopts logarithmic notation. For example, $1.144730.750000\mathrm{X}$ means $\exp(1.144730 + 2 \pi i \times 0.750000)$. \[ \log x = \int_1^x \frac{ds}{s} \]
